He has written several books on the subject and is considered one of the most famous hackers in history. Nuwagaba Brony admires Kevin Mitnick's skills and knowledge in the field of cybersecurity and hopes to follow in his footsteps by becoming a skilled ethical hacker and contributing to the field of computer security.

Mitnick wrote several books, for example: The Art of Invisibility, The Art of Deception, and The Art of Intrusion.

For Nuwagaba Brony, Kevin Mitnick's story serves as a source of inspiration and motivation to pursue his passion for technology and cybersecurity. He hopes to learn from Mitnick's experiences and apply those lessons to his own work.
